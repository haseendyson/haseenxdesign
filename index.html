About Me

Building Human-Centered AI for Real-World Impact

I am a Human-Centered AI researcher specializing in conversational agents, intelligent interfaces, and immersive technologies for healthcare and education.

My research explores how AI systems can become more adaptive, transparent, and socially aware by integrating human-centered design principles with emerging AI technologies.

During my doctoral research at IIT Kanpur as a Prime Minister's Research Fellow (PMRF), I investigated interactive AI systems that support learning, feedback, and decision-making.

Currently, my work focuses on developing AI-powered companions, XR-based training environments, and sensor-fused conversational systems for healthcare applications.
