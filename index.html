Research

My work spans human-centered design, artificial intelligence, and healthcare — with a focus on making both systems and services usable in resource-constrained contexts.

HCAI

Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence

Studying how AI systems can be designed around real user needs rather than technical capability alone.

Healthcare Design

Healthcare Infrastructure & Services Design

Designing deployable, frugal healthcare infrastructure for underserved and low-resource communities.

Design Theory

(More-than) Human-Centered Design

Extending design methods beyond the individual user to social, ecological, and systemic considerations.

Emerging Tech

Computational Design & XR

Exploring extended reality and computational tools as part of the design and prototyping process.

HCI

Human-Computer Interaction

Understanding how people interact with interfaces and systems across cultural and infrastructural contexts.

Design for Society

Frugal, Behavioral & Critical Design

Working on frugal innovation, behavioral design, and design education for global health challenges.

See. Feel. Act. Good design starts by understanding the people and systems we're designing for — especially when they're the ones furthest from access.
